My research interests lie broadly in the intersections of Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Visualization (VIS) and Extended Reality (XR/AR/VR), with a particular focus on immersive data visualization and interaction. Through my research, I aim to design next-generation immersive interfaces and spatial computing systems that fundamentally transform how humans perceive and interact with data.

I am particularly interested in:

  • Interaction with Data Visualization: designing human-data interaction tools for data visualization.
  • Interaction Techniques in AR/VR: creating efficient interaction methods for immersive applications.
  • Interactive Human-AI Experiences: exploring ways to enhance human-AI interaction.

Previously, I completed my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) where I was advised by Prof. Xiaojuan Ma.

We welcome BS/MS students and visiting interns/scholars. Please fill this Google form, if your are interested in research about HCI, XR/AR/VR and Data Visualization.
